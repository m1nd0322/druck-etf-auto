# Hybrid 5 Rotation Rationale (2026-04-22)

This note explains the selected 5-ETF hybrid live-rotation sets for Korea and the US.

## Goal

Use a minimal but usable live-rotation universe that is:
- stable enough to survive risk-off periods
- simple enough to explain to other people
- aggressive enough to participate in strong risk-on phases
- compact enough to reduce duplicated exposures

## Korea hybrid 5

- `069500.KS` — core Korea equity beta
- `091160.KS` — growth / semiconductor leadership
- `114260.KS` — defensive KR bond sleeve
- `132030.KS` — gold hedge / alternative sleeve
- `122630.KS` — offensive leveraged Korea equity sleeve

### Why this set

- `069500.KS` anchors the portfolio to the broad Korean market.
- `091160.KS` captures the most structurally important domestic growth leadership bucket.
- `114260.KS` provides a local defensive parking asset instead of forcing cash-only behavior.
- `132030.KS` gives crisis diversification that is different from simple equity/bond rotation.
- `122630.KS` adds controlled offensive upside in risk-on phases without turning the entire universe into a leveraged-only basket.

## US hybrid 5

- `SPY` — core US equity beta
- `SMH` — growth / semiconductor leadership
- `TLT` — defensive long-duration bond sleeve
- `GLD` — gold hedge / alternative sleeve
- `QLD` — offensive leveraged Nasdaq sleeve

### Why this set

- `SPY` is the cleanest broad-market anchor.
- `SMH` captures leadership when growth and semiconductors dominate risk-on phases.
- `TLT` provides a classic risk-off pivot.
- `GLD` diversifies against equity and bond regime stress.
- `QLD` introduces offense without going all the way to the most extreme leveraged profile.

## Portfolio design principle

These sets are intentionally hybrid:
- 3 sleeves are stability-oriented (core / defense / alternative)
- 1 sleeve expresses structural growth leadership
- 1 sleeve expresses tactical offense

This keeps the universe understandable and tradable while still allowing the strategy to become more aggressive in strong conditions.
